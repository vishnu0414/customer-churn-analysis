# 📌 Overview

The **Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive **Power BI** project designed to analyze customer churn behavior in a telecommunications or subscription-based business.

The dashboard enables businesses to identify:

- 👥 Customer demographics
- 📉 Churn trends
- 💰 Revenue patterns
- 📞 Customer support behavior
- 📅 Contract preferences
- ⭐ High-risk customers

The goal is to help organizations make **data-driven decisions** that improve customer retention.

# 📈 Business Insights

<details>

<summary><b>📊 Customer Insights</b></summary>

### 👥 Customer Behavior

- Customers with longer tenure are less likely to churn.
- Monthly contracts have higher churn than annual contracts.
- Premium customers show better retention.

</details>

<details>

<summary><b>💰 Revenue Insights</b></summary>

- High spending customers contribute significantly to revenue.
- Payment delays are associated with increased churn.
- Customer lifetime value increases with tenure.

</details>

<details>

<summary><b>📞 Support Analysis</b></summary>

- Frequent support calls indicate dissatisfaction.
- Customers with repeated complaints have higher churn probability.
- Support quality directly impacts retention.

</details>
